Payback Methods

How payback methods work
Payback methods are how you prefer to be paid back — a bank wire, a Bitcoin address, Pix, PayPal, Venmo, Wise, and more. Sensitive fields (account numbers, wallet addresses) are encrypted locally and stay masked in this list until you unlock them with your auth code. Each method carries a share policy so you control whether a contact asking how to settle up can receive it automatically, gets asked first, or never sees it.
