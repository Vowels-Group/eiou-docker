About Transaction Details
Everything recorded for this transaction — who it was between, when it was committed, the signed amount, and the identifiers that let both sides verify it without trusting a third party.

Transaction ID is the cryptographic fingerprint of this specific transaction; both sides' chains must agree on it for the balance to be considered settled. Previous Tx links to the transaction directly before this one in your chain with this counterparty — clicking it opens that transaction's details. Walking the chain backwards lets you reconstruct the full history between you and a contact.

For P2P routed transactions (multi-hop payments where your node was a relay or an endpoint), you'll also see the end recipient, the routing fee you earned or paid, and the hop sequence. For contact requests, the transaction itself is the handshake that established the relationship.
