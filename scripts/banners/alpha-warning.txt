OPEN ALPHA

eIOU is a decentralized peer-to-peer credit network. There is
no central server, no central authority, and no central point
of failure. Each node is independently operated and stores its
own data.

This software is in open alpha. It is under active development
and updates may change how features work without prior notice.

IMPORTANT:

IOUs created on the network are bilateral agreements between
real people. Once accepted by both parties, they may not be
reversible. Treat every IOU as a real financial obligation.

eIOU tracks debt in any unit of account chosen by its users
(fiat currencies, commodities, time, or custom units) and is
not a cryptocurrency, token, or digital asset. No coins are
mined, minted, or generated. The software facilitates the
recording and routing of personal credit obligations only.

You are responsible for:
  1. Securing your own node and private keys
  2. Maintaining your own backups
  3. Understanding the obligations you create
  4. Complying with all applicable laws in your jurisdiction

Do not use this for obligations you cannot afford to lose.

Report issues: https://github.com/eiou-org/eiou-docker
